Coach App — Design Directions

DIRECTION 1

Minimal Zen

Maximum breathing room. Conversations float on pure white. The coach's words have space to land. For users who want calm over stimulation.

Key Characteristics

  • Ultra-minimal chrome — No borders on coach messages, content floats
  • Maximum whitespace — Messages have room to breathe
  • Dark user bubbles — Black instead of terracotta, more neutral
  • Best for: Users who find typical apps overstimulating
  • Risk: May feel too sparse, less cozy warmth

DIRECTION 4

Soft Focus

Gentle gradients and soft shadows. Everything feels slightly blurred at the edges. Like looking at the world through morning light.

Key Characteristics

  • Gradient background — Subtle warm-to-warmer gradient
  • Very rounded bubbles — 24px radius, softer than standard
  • Soft drop shadows — Diffuse shadows create depth without edges
  • Pattern insight treatment — Shows cross-session observation
  • Best for: Maximum softness, dreamy quality
  • Risk: Gradients can feel dated if not executed carefully

Key Characteristics

  • Accent-filled starters — Peach backgrounds make them feel substantial
  • Bold icon treatment — Filled accent icon, not outline
  • Confident copy — "Ready when you are" vs passive greetings
  • Best for: Users who want the coach to feel present and confident
  • Risk: May feel too directive for users who want softer entry
